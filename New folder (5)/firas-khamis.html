Dr. Firas Khamis

Dr. Firas Khamis is certified in internal medicine, pulmonary, and critical care. He graduated from the University of Minnesota Medical School and completed his internal medicine program at the University of Minnesota in 2012. In 2021, Dr. Khamis completed his pulmonary and critical care fellowship at the University of South Alabama Medical Center.

Dr. Khamis joined Nevada Critical Care Consultants in August 2021. He has extensive experience in treating pulmonary and critical care conditions and has multiple hospital affiliations, including Henderson Hospital, Summerlin Hospital, and Centennial Hospital.
